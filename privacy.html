Privacy

At Mue we care about your privacy. That's why in the extension, website and API we do not track you at all. Everything is opensource under MIT (excluding the extension which is BSD-3.0 icons which are CC-BY-4.0) for transparency purposes. The only third parties involved with running the software are as follows: Cloudflare (our DNS and DDOS protection provider), DigitalOcean (our hosting provider), Google (Chrome store hosting), Firefox (Firefox store hosting) and GitHub (code hosting).
